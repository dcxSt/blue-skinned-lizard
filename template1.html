ABOUT THE COLLECTIVE

Blue Lizard is a punk collective dedicated to amplifying marginalized voices through radical publishing and direct action. We distribute DIY zines, organize shows, and build community resistance against corporate media monopolies. Our mission: Keep the underground alive, support independent creators, and destroy the status quo through information warfare.
